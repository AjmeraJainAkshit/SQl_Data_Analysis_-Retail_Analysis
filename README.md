# Retail Sales Analysis Using MySQL

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data using SQL in MySQL.  
The analysis includes data cleaning, exploration, and solving real-world business problems to generate meaningful insights from retail transaction data.

---

# Business Problems Solved

## 1. Sales on a Specific Date
Retrieved all sales made on `2022-11-05`.

## 2. Clothing Transactions Analysis
Filtered Clothing category transactions with quantity greater than 4 during November 2022.

## 3. Total Sales by Category
Calculated total sales and total orders for each category.

## 4. Average Customer Age
Found average customer age for Beauty category purchases.

## 5. High Value Transactions
Retrieved transactions where total sales exceeded 1000.

## 6. Transactions by Gender & Category
Analyzed total transactions grouped by category and gender.

## 7. Best Selling Month Each Year
Used window functions and ranking to identify the highest average sales month per year.

## 8. Top 5 Customers
Identified top customers based on total sales amount.

## 9. Unique Customers by Category
Calculated distinct customer counts for each category.

## 10. Sales Shift Analysis
Categorized orders into:
- Morning
- Afternoon
- Evening

and counted total orders in each shift.
